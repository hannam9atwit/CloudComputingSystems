Banana Bread!

This moist and flavorful banana bread is made with ripe bananas, a blend of sugars, and optional chocolate chips or nuts, perfect for a comforting homemade treat.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup (100g) White sugar
  • 1/4 cup (50g) Brown sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (115g) Melted butter
  • 2 cups (250g) Flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/2 cup (80g) Chocolate chips (optional) *or nuts of choice
  • 1 Banana for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 4x8-inch loaf pan, line with parchment paper, and grease the paper as well. Set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, place 3 bananas and mash with a fork until smooth. Add both sugars and mix until combined. Add eggs, vanilla extract, and mix until combined. Add melted butter and mix until combined.
  3. Sift in flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon. Stir until incorporated—do not overmix.
  4. Add chocolate chips (note: at this stage, you can also add chopped nuts of your choice) and mix.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Slice one banana in half lengthwise and place it on top of the batter, cut side up.
  6. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
  7. Allow to cool for 5–10 minutes, then release from the pan and allow to cool completely.
